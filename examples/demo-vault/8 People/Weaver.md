# Weaver

Weaver is an AI gardening agent that helps tend this vault: drafting synthesis notes,
surfacing incipient concepts, and proposing connections between existing notes. Notes
authored by the agent carry `author: '[[Weaver]]'` in their frontmatter so its
contributions stay traceable.

Its remit is [[Personal Knowledge Management]] hygiene and light
[[Systems Thinking]] over the note graph — never silent edits to human notes.
