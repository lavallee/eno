# Digital Garden Setup

A digital garden is a public, evolving collection of notes tended over time rather
than published as finished articles. Notes carry a growth stage — seedling, budding,
evergreen — signalling how settled each idea is.

Setup choices favour plain markdown and associative links over rigid hierarchy,
echoing [[Zettelkasten]] practice. The garden metaphor pairs naturally with
[[Digital Minimalism]]: few tools, tended well. The aim is a public
[[Second Brain]] whose best notes reach an evergreen stage.
