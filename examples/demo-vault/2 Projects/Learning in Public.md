# Learning in Public

Notes toward a habit of publishing what I learn as I learn it, rather than waiting
for polish that never comes.

## The wager

Publishing early invites correction and forces clarity — you cannot hide a shaky idea
behind private shorthand. It leans on [[Personal Knowledge Management]] to have
something ready to share, and on [[Systems Thinking]] to reason about the feedback
loop between audience and understanding. The output surface is a
[[Digital Garden Setup]], distilled with [[Progressive Summarization]]. It is also an
exercise in [[Digital Minimalism]]: publish the signal, drop the rest. Writing for a
[[Future Self]] and writing for strangers turn out to demand the same honesty.
