# Time Blocking

Time blocking assigns every part of the working day to a specific task in advance,
turning an open-ended to-do list into a concrete schedule. The plan will not survive
contact with reality intact, and that is fine — its job is to force choices about
priority before the day's noise arrives.

Blocks protect deep work by making a [[Flow State]] more likely: a defended hour is
long enough to get past the friction of starting. A [[Pomodoro Timer]] is a finer
tool inside a block for sustaining attention through the dull middle.
